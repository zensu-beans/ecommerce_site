Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Orders over $50 ship free. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available at checkout for an additional fee.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Simply contact us and we'll arrange a pickup.

Are the products covered by warranty?

Yes — all products come with a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty. Many items include an extended 2-year warranty through our store at no extra cost.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also check your order status from your account page anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, GCash, Maya, and bank transfers. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.
